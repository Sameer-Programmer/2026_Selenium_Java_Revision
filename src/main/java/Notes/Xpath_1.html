================================================= XPATH REVISION NOTES ================================================= 1) Relative XPath (Single Attribute) ------------------------------------ Syntax: //tagname[text()='value'] Example: //a[text()='Bestsellers'] 2) Relative XPath (Multiple Attributes) ---------------------------------------- Syntax: //tagname[@attribute='value'][text()='text'] Example: //a[@tabindex='0'][text()='Bestsellers'] 3) AND Operator --------------- Syntax: //tagname[@attribute1='value1' and @attribute2='value2'] Examples: //a[@tabindex='0' and text()='Bestsellers'] //img[@class='s-image' and @alt="Max Men's Cotton Regular Fit T-Shirt"] 4) OR Operator -------------- Syntax: //tagname[@attribute1='value1' or @attribute2='value2'] Examples: //a[@tabindex='0' or text()='Bestsellers'] //img[@class='s-image' or @alt="Max Men's Cotton Regular Fit T-Shirt"] 5) contains() ------------- Syntax: //tagname[contains(@attribute,'value')] Examples: //img[contains(@alt,"Max Men's Cotton Regular Fit T-Shirt")] //input[contains(@id,'user')] Notes: - Used when partial text is known. - '=' operator is not used inside contains(). 6) starts-with() ---------------- Syntax: //tagname[starts-with(@attribute,'value')] Examples: //img[starts-with(@alt,"Max Men's Cotton Regular Fit T-Shirt")] //input[starts-with(@id,'user')] Notes: - Checks whether the attribute starts with the given value. 7) text() --------- Syntax: //tagname[text()='value'] Examples: //a[text()='Bestsellers'] //button[text()='Login'] Notes: - Exact text match. - Most commonly used for links and buttons. ================================================= MOST COMMON INTERVIEW XPATHS ================================================= //a[text()='Login'] //input[contains(@id,'user')] //input[starts-with(@id,'user')] //input[@type='text' and @name='email'] //input[@id='email' or @name='email']
